FAQ's

How to register ?

Go to Register Tab and fill up the provided form there. Once you fill the Registration Form, you will receive a confirmation mail on the entered email id in form in 10-15mins.
Please Note that you need to fill this form only once.


How do I lend or borrow a book if I am a registered user ?

Go to Books Tab, select a book you want to borrow/lend and click on the respective button. Fill the provided form and you will receive a confirmation mail in your inbox. You will then receive the details of the borrower/lender whom you need to contact and collect/give the book.
For more details please refer to How it works Tab


How do I set up a meeting with the person that I want to borrow/lend a book from/to ?

After you have successfully filled the Google Form provided after selecting the book, you will receive the contact details of the borrower/lender. You then need to meet them and exchange the book.
For more details please refer to How it works Tab


How secure are my contact details and what level of privacy is ensured by your website ?

Please note that your information is directly updated to Google Spreadsheet which is handled by the Biteabook Team. This way, your information is completely safe with us and cannot be misused.
Please make sure that you don not allow anyone else your details to lend or borrow books on your name. This can get you into trouble.


How is your website benefiting from the service you are providing ?

Biteabook is currently a non-profit organisation which is currently not making any kind of revenue by providing the borrow/lend facility.
